## Key Points to Emphasise

1. **It's agentic:** 10 agents, not 1 API call
2. **Transparent:** Every decision is logged and visible
3. **Multilingual:** Works in Urdu, Roman Urdu, English, mixed — even with typos
4. **Not just a list:** Multi-factor ranking, dynamic pricing, recovery options
5. **Demo-reliable:** Fully offline — no network dependency during presentation
