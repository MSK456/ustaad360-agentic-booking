# Product Demo Walkthrough Guide
1. **Welcome Screen**: Select guest or complete profile registration.
2. **Search Input**: Type Roman Urdu phrases (e.g. "urgent AC kharab hai").
3. **Intent Review**: Inspect detailsParsed and correct coordinate locations.
4. **Provider List**: View ranked ustaads, checkout the "Why not closest?" card, examine trust indicators.
5. **Provider Detail Screen**: Check out the FairPrice meter and review exact budget mismatches.
6. **Booking Confirmation**: Choose payment channels, review overlap warnings.
7. **Follow-Up Screen**: Tap "Progress Status" simulator to watch the status transition through all 8 timeline indicators.
