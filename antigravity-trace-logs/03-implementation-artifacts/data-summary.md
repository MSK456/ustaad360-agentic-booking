# Data Catalogs
- **Providers Pool**: Includes 12 realistic mock tradespeople located in specific sectors (G-13, F-10, DHA Lahore, Gulberg) with varying pricing, rating, cancellation metrics.
- **Groceries / Essentials Catalog**: Contains realistic per-unit pricing for fruits, fresh meat, dairy items, and basic pantry goods.
- **Privacy Compliance**: All entries are purely synthetic. No real client details or real-world addresses are required.
